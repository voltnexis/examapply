1. Eligibility Checklist

Age Criteria

  • You must be at least 17 years old as of December 31st of the exam year (2026).
  • There is no upper age limit for the examination.

Academic Qualification

  • Must have passed Class 12 or equivalent.
  • Students appearing in Class 12 in 2026 are also eligible.
  • There is no limit on the number of attempts.

Mandatory Subjects & Marks

  • Must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Biotechnology in Class 12.
  • Minimum Aggregate Marks (P, C, B/Bt) are:
    • 50% for General/EWS
    • 40% for SC/ST/OBC (NCL)

3. The 4-Stage Application Process

1

Registration (Part 1)

Generate your Application Number using your name, mother's name, father's name, date of birth, and valid Mobile Number and Email ID. Create a secure password.

2

Fill Application Form (Part 2)

Provide all detailed personal, academic (including your 11th and 12th details using the specific NEET Examination Code), and contact information. Select your preferred exam cities (usually 4 options).

3

Upload Scanned Images

Upload all required documents: Two Photographs (Passport & Postcard), Signature, Fingerprint Impression, and Class 10 Certificate. Use the specified formats and size ranges (refer to Section 2).

4

Fee Payment & Confirmation

Pay the examination fee online. Upon successful transaction, download and print the Confirmation Page. This page is your final proof of application and should be securely saved.
