1. Eligibility Checklist

Age Criteria

  • There is no age limit for candidates appearing in CUET UG.
  • However, candidates must satisfy the age criteria (if any) of the specific University/Institution they are applying to.

Academic Qualification

  • Must have passed Class 12 or equivalent examination.
  • Candidates appearing in the 2026 qualifying examination are also eligible.
  • There is no limit on the number of attempts a candidate can take.

Marks & Subject Mapping

  • The Minimum Marks required are determined by the respective university. CUET sets no universal minimum marks.
  • You MUST appear in the same subject in CUET that you studied at the Class 12 level (as per the university's program-specific requirements).

3. The 3-Stage CUET Application Process

1

Online Registration

Sign up using your basic details (Name, DOB, Mobile/Email). This generates your unique Application Number, which is required for all future steps. Use a secure password.

2

Fill Form, University/Subject Selection, & Upload

Fill in detailed personal and academic information. Crucially, select the Universities and the specific subject combinations/Domain Papers you wish to appear for, based on your desired course eligibility. Upload Photograph and Signature.

3

Fee Payment & Confirmation Page

The fee is calculated based on the number of subjects you selected. Pay the fee online. After successful payment, download and print the Confirmation Page, which signifies the final completion of your application.
