1. Are You Eligible?

Year & Attempts

  • You must have passed Class 12 in 2024, 2025, or be appearing in 2026.
  • No upper age limit to appear for the exam.
  • You get a maximum of 3 consecutive years (6 sessions) to appear for the exam.

Marks (For NIT/IIIT Admission)

*Note: There are no minimum marks required just to appear in JEE Main itself.*

  • General/OBC/EWS: 75% aggregate in Class 12 OR be in the top 20 percentile of your board.
  • SC/ST: 65% aggregate in Class 12.

Mandatory Subjects in Class 12

  • B.E. / B.Tech: Physics + Mathematics + (Any one from Chemistry / Biology / Tech Vocational Subject)
  • B.Arch: Mathematics + Physics + Chemistry
  • B.Planning: Mathematics Only

4. The 5-Step Application Process

1

Online Registration

Visit the official NTA JEE Main website. Use your Email ID and Mobile Number to register. You will receive a system-generated Application Numberâ€”save this immediately!

2

Fill Application Form

Log in with your Application Number and Password. Fill in all personal (Aadhaar details, address), academic (Class 10/12 details), and exam-related information (preferred course, exam city choices, medium).

3

Upload Documents

Upload the scanned images of your Photo, Signature, and Class 10 Certificate. Make sure they match the size/format requirements (refer to Section 2).

4

Pay Examination Fee

Proceed to the payment gateway. Pay the required fee (see Section 3) using an online method. Keep the payment receipt.

5

Download Confirmation Page

After successful payment, download and print the Confirmation Page. This is proof of your submission and is vital for future reference.

Pro Tip:

Always double-check all details *before* final submission. NTA provides a correction window, but it is limited!
